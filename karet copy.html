Karet Study

Located in the centre of one of Jakarta's busiest business areas, Karet is home to a large kampung community

The Cost of Living: The roots economy's role in providing food, drink and somewhere safe to sleep

For our first piece of independent research demonstrating the benefits of mixed income living, we focus on the Karet neighbourhood; a strategic location in the centre of one of Jakarta's busiest business areas, and also home to a large kampung community. The ways in which commercial life in this area and the interior settlement interact and influence each other will be the core of this investigation.
